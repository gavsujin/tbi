About TBI Center

The Technology Business Incubator (TBI) Center at Caraga State University is dedicated to fostering innovation and entrepreneurship in the region. We provide comprehensive support to aspiring entrepreneurs and startups through our state-of-the-art facilities and expert guidance.

Our mission is to transform innovative ideas into successful businesses that contribute to regional economic development and create sustainable employment opportunities.
